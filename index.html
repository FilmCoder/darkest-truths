Yesterday I couldn't stop crying because I got rejected.
For 20 minutes I sat there frozen, chin in my hands and a tight knot in my throat. I was staring at the blank wall inches from my face, stuck, unable to shift my gaze even a few degrees to either direction. Finally, I lifted myself and did what needed to be done. For me at least, 20 minutes to get over a rejection that big is a new record, and I'm proud of myself for it.
I think sometimes we hide, we fake, we pretend.
This page is about honesty, about being vulnerable. Every day you visit you'll see a new piece of me. I want to be honest to every stranger and every friend, to listen to them, to trust them - even with my hopes and insecurities.
